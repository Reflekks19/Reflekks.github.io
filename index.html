Darksiders 3

Darksiders 3 has announced that the game will be played as Fury, the sister of the Four Horsemen. Who was sent out to "restore the balance between good and evel on Earth". Harnessing her magic, players can "unleash" Fury's multiple forms, each form gives the players new abilities, moves, and weaponry. Darksiders 3 is going to be "open-ending", "Living", and "free-form". Players will have to explore the map in order to advance to the next part of the story. The game is developed by former Vigil Games employees. The same team that created Darksiders and the sequel Darksiders 2. The official release date is not yet certain, but we can expect Darksiders 3 to be ready to release in 2018. It will be available for PC, PS4, and Xbox One.
